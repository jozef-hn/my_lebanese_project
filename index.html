Welcome to Lebanon

Explore the rich and diverse past of one of the world's oldest civilizations.

Let's get "Lebanesed":

Lebanon is like nature’s overachieving student—packed into a space just over 10,000 square kilometers, it boasts snow-capped mountains, sunny Mediterranean beaches, and forests so dense they once gave rise to the legendary Cedars of Lebanon. Scientists might call it a biodiversity hotspot, but locals just call it home. It’s a place where tectonic plates meet (hello, earthquakes!), where ancient Phoenician ruins coexist with modern skyscrapers, and where even the food seems to defy physics—how can something as small as a tabbouleh bowl pack so much flavor? If you’re looking for a country that’s both a geological marvel and a culinary playground, Lebanon delivers with a wink and a plate of hummus.

Bored? Take a Look at our Culture and History!

Lebanese culture blends ancient traditions with modern charm. Hospitality is key—guests are always welcomed with coffee and sweets. Dance, music, and festivals bring people together, celebrating life with joy and flair.
